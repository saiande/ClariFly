Welcome to Customer Help! Below are some helpful tips in order to navigate through the customer view of Clarifly. Each subsection of this page is each tab on the navigation bar and has guidelines on what kind of information you can find in each section. HOME: In this section,you can enter information and we will find the best flights available for you. MY ACCOUNT: In this section, you can edit your account information. MY RESERVATIONS: In this section, you can view all of your past and current reservations and you have the option to put them into the auction. MY AUCTIONS: Shows you all of the auctions you can and are participating in. You can bet on auctions in order to win a flight. HELP: Displays this help menu.
