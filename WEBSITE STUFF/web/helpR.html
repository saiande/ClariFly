Welcome to Customer Representative Help! Below are some helpful tips in order to navigate through the manager view of Clarifly. Each subsection of this page is each tab on the navigation bar and has guidelines on what kind of information you can find in each section. RESERVATIONS: In this section, you have to enter a customer’s account number, the airport name he is flying from, the airport name he is flying to, his departing date, the number of tickets to be purchased, the airline name, flight number, and leg number. Then you will be redirected to a page indicating if the reservation was successfully made. CUSTOMER INFO: When you click on this link, you will be redirected to a page where you can edit the customer’s information except his account number. MAILING LIST: When you click on this link, you will be redirected to a page where you will be given the name of all the customers using clariFLY, their physical address, and their email address. FLIGHT SUGGESTION: When you click on this link you will be redirected to a page with a flight suggestion list sorted by the airports you visited before. EMPLOYEE INFO: When you click on this link, you will be redirected to a page where a table of all the employees in clariFLY is displayed. The customer representative will not be able to edit any of the employees’ information. All the employees’ information will be displayed except his hourly rate. Customer Representatives should not be able to perform manager-level transactions such as editing employees’ information or deleting and adding users.
