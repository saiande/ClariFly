Welcome to Manager Help! Below are some helpful tips in order to navigate through the manager view of Clarifly. Each subsection of this page is each tab on the navigation bar and has guidelines on what kind of information you can find in each section. CUSTOMERS: Add Customer When you click on this link, you will be redirected to a page where you can record all the information we need in order to add a customer account to the database. Delete Customer Next to this section, you will find a text box where you can insert the customer’s account number and based on this information the customer will be deleted form the database (along with their reservations). Customers on a Flight In this section, when you insert a Flight Number and Airline ID you will be taken to a page where a table of all the customers on a flight will be displayed. EMPLOYEE: Add Employee When you click on this link, you will be redirected to a page where you can record all the information we need in order to add an employee account to the database. Delete Employee Next to this section, you will find a text box where you can insert the employee’s ID number and based on this information the employee will be deleted form the database. Edit Employee In this section, when you insert the employee’s ID number you will be redirected to a page where all of the employee’s information will be displayed in textboxes and the manager can change the fields as necessary. Then click submit and the changes will be made to the database. SALES: Highest Revenue (Customer) When you click on this link you will be redirected to a page where a table of the customers with the most revenue will be displayed from highest to lowest revenue. Highest Revenue (Employee) When you click on this link you will be redirected to a page where a table of the employees with the most revenue will be displayed from highest to lowest revenue. Revenue (Flight) In this section, you will see two textboxes with spaces for Flight Number and Airline ID. After entering these details you will be redirected to a page where a table of customers with reservations on this particular flight are displayed. Revenue (Destination City) In this section, you will see a textbox for destination city. After entering this detail you will be redirected to a page where the total revenue made for a destination city is displayed. Revenue (Customer) In this section after you enter the customer Account Number, you will be redirected to a page where the total revenue made form this customer will be displayed. FLIGHTS: List of all Flights When you click on this link, you will be redirected to a page where a table of all flights available is displayed. Most Active Flights When you click on this link, you will be redirected to a page where a table of the most active flights is displayed. Flights (Airport) In this section when you type in the Airport ID and press submit, you will be redirected to a page where a table of all flights in this particular airport is displayed. RESERVATIONS: Reservations (Flight) In this section, you have to enter the Flight Number and the Airline ID. Then you will be redirected to a page where a table of all of the reservations for a particular flight is displayed. Reservations (Customer) In this section, when you enter the Customer Account Number you will be redirected to a page where a table of all reservations made by this one customer is displayed.
